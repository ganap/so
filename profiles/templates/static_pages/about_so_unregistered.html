How do I request a second opinion?


Outlined is the process to ensure that we receive all of the information needed to create comprehensive second opinion report.

To request an online second opinion from Hygeiais, you will first need to register. Once registered, using our secure site you will asked to enter your prior diagnosis and continue.


1. Requestor and Patient Information

Patients are able to authorize a requestor to complete the Hygeiais second opinion process on their behalf.

2. Medical Questionnaires

Along with completing two questionnaires regarding your general medical history and history related to your diagnosis, you also will be able to submit the top three questions you would like answered in the second opinion report.

3. Required Materials

In this step, you will collect and submit all of the required medical records, images, test results and consent forms. Hygeiais coordinators can help you get your medical records sent to us. In some cases, we can directly query your physicians EMR system to obtain you health record if you grant us permission and your physician uses a interoperable EMR.

5. Payment

Due to nationwide insurance regulations regarding second opinions, the Hygeais program is not covered by insurance or Medicare. We accept payment by most major credit cards or certified U.S. bank check or money order.

6. Final Submission

You will be able to review all of the information you have submitted prior to sending your request to the corresponding expert
