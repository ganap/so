Patient


  • Up to date information

  • Get organized

  • Care team

Learn about your condition, treatment options and medications

Accurate up to date information gives you the confidence to ask your physician and other members of the healthcare team the right questions. This results in better outcome for your care. You can also find latest research publications and news articles and genetic information about your condition. In addition, hygeiais helps you find matching clinical trials and connect with the investigators running the trial.

Organize your health and medical information

You can manage all your of health and medical related content in one place, which can be shared with your care team without going through the hassle of calling your doctor's office every time.

  • Record your vitals, moods, side effects and treatment details daily.
  • Set reminders for your appointments and medications.
  • Maintain a calendar of medications and other events.
  • Receive your doctor visit summaries automatically or upload those copies.
  • Aggregate data from all your health and medical devices into one central place.
  • Export summary of your health and medical information and provide them for a doctor who cannot access your hygeiais health summary.

Engage with your care team

Engaging with your Doctor and healthcare team to give them a complete picture of your heath and medical history leads to better outcomes and fewer errors.

Sharing your health information electronically helps you obtain second opinion from top experts anywhere in the world in a matter of few days. In addition, this helps your care team to be on the cutting edge of treatment protocols.

Health Professional


  • Community support

  • Second opinions

Get support and encouragement from the community

Get support from the community of other patients and discussion forums and families who share similar experience as yours. Sharing your story helps someone who was just diagnosed with a similar condition in navigating through the disease.

Obtain a second opinion from a leading expert for your condition

Hygeiais with its proprietary algorithm has built a directory of experts specifically treating your condition. We help you get a second opinion from some of these experts who are conducting research in this area at leading institutions in the world. The Hygeias team of healthcare professionals is there to support you every step of the way, from scheduling appointments, collecting and digitizing your medical records, and following up on your care to make sure you are on the right path. In most cases, you can connect with the specialist via a video call and get your questions answered.
